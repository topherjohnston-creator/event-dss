# ⚠️ DISCLAIMER

## EXPERIMENTAL, NON-OPERATIONAL, RESEARCH PURPOSES ONLY

This Event Decision Support System (DSS) uses **experimental, non-operational** forecast data from NOAA's Regional Ensemble Forecast System (REFS). 

### What This System Is

✅ Research tool for weather analysis
✅ Educational resource for forecast interpretation
✅ Event planning support system
✅ Prototype decision aid

### What This System Is NOT

❌ **NOT** operational weather forecast
❌ **NOT** approved for official decisions
❌ **NOT** suitable for safety-critical operations
❌ **NOT** a replacement for NWS forecasts

## Data Source: REFS

- **Status**: Experimental prototype (as of May 2026)
- **Bucket**: `noaa-rrfs-pds` on AWS S3
- **Approval**: Not approved for operational use
- **Documentation**: https://vlab.noaa.gov/web/rrfs/

REFS is still in development and may have data quality issues, missing cycles, or unexpected behavior.

## Do NOT Use For

- ❌ Official airport operations decisions
- ❌ Safety-critical event planning
- ❌ FAA compliance or reporting
- ❌ Official NWS product replacement
- ❌ Insurance or liability decisions
- ❌ Anything requiring regulatory approval

## Use Instead

For **official forecasts**, consult:

- **National Weather Service**: https://weather.gov
- **Aviation Weather Center**: https://aviationweather.gov
- **Local NWS Office**: https://weather.gov/rev/ (Reno)
- **Airport Operations**: Contact KRNO directly
